# nucleos: Central Bank of Brazil Inflation Cores

Calculates the inflation cores and other analytical series derived from
the IPCA, following the consolidated methodology published by the
Central Bank of Brazil in Technical Note 57 (December 2025). Covers the
22 series published in the SGS, from January 1991 onwards, spanning the
five IPCA structures.
